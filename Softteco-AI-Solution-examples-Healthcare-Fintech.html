Revenue Cycle

AI billing audit platform

Automated review of medical claims before submission and after denial. The system reads claim lines, coding, and supporting documentation, flags inconsistencies against payer rules, and produces an explainable audit trail for every recommendation. A billing specialist confirms or overrides — the model never files on its own.

Architected region-agnostic: payer rulesets and code systems are pluggable, so the same core serves US clients today and other markets without a rewrite. Deployable in the cloud or fully on-premise, with no PHI leaving the client perimeter.

OUTCOMEFewer denials and less rework — coding issues are caught before the claim is filed, not after rejection.
LLM claims reasoning Payer-policy retrieval Human-in-the-loop On-prem option

Vertical 02

Fintech

In financial systems the constraints are different — market data licensing, transaction integrity, and latency budgets measured in milliseconds. We build on that foundation: our team has prior experience in CIS banking infrastructure and integrates directly with institutional data sources such as Bloomberg.

Commodity Trading

Commodity trading & deal management platform

An end-to-end platform for physical commodity trading: deal capture, counterparty management, document flow, and pricing against live market data via a Bloomberg B-PIPE integration with per-exchange entitlement handling. On top of the platform, an autonomous deal agent — a LangGraph-orchestrated workflow — drafts deal structures, checks them against trading rules, and routes them to traders for approval. A scoped, versioned public API exposes the platform to partner systems.

OUTCOMEFaster deal cycles with controls intact — the agent prepares and validates, traders keep the final decision.
Bloomberg B-PIPE Exchange entitlements (EID) Autonomous deal agent Trader approval gates Partner API
Retail Banking

Voice banking suite for MENA & CIS

An embedded voice agent for retail banking apps: balances, card operations, transfers, and product questions — by voice, in the customer's language. Built on a structured schema of 60 banking intents, each mapped to whitelisted backend operations, so the agent acts only within defined, auditable boundaries.

OUTCOMEVoice self-service without operational risk — every action the agent can take is bounded, logged, and reviewable.
60 structured intents Schema-bound actions Multilingual
Trading Analytics

Trading analytics & backtesting platform

A CFD trading analytics environment combining institutional market data, an event-driven backtesting engine, and high-performance charting for desk-side analysis — built for analysts who need to move from hypothesis to backtested result inside one tool, with the same data feeding research and live monitoring.

Data ingestion, strategy execution, and visualization are separated architecturally, so each layer scales and evolves independently.

Foundation

One infrastructure underneath

Both verticals run on inference infrastructure we design and operate ourselves. For healthcare this means data sovereignty; for fintech, predictable latency and cost. Clients are not locked to a third-party model provider.

Production GPU inference cluster

A self-hosted vLLM serving cluster running open-weight models in production, with capacity planning, monitoring, and failover handled by our team.

Orchestration & scheduling

GPU workload orchestration built on HashiCorp Nomad with a FastAPI control plane — models are scheduled, scaled, and rotated without downtime.

Model optimization

Quantization (AWQ, INT4, FP8), speculative decoding, and tensor parallelism applied per workload — the difference between a demo and an economically viable product.

Sovereign & on-premise deployment

The full stack — models, pipelines, orchestration — can be deployed inside a client's perimeter or in a chosen jurisdiction, including air-gapped environments.
